Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 6, 2025

1. Introduction

SmartCookBook ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Email address and authentication credentials when you create an account
  • Recipe Data: Recipes, ingredients, and notes you create within the app
  • Invoice Data: Supplier invoices you upload for processing
  • QuickBooks Connection: OAuth tokens to connect to your QuickBooks account (we do not store your QuickBooks password)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Process invoices and sync data with QuickBooks
  • Store your recipes and kitchen management data
  • Improve and optimize our application

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services:

  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We use industry-standard security measures
  • QuickBooks OAuth tokens are stored securely in Firebase Firestore

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

  • QuickBooks Online: For accounting and bill management (governed by Intuit's privacy policy)
  • Google Firebase: For authentication and data storage
  • Claude AI (Anthropic): For invoice parsing (invoice text is processed but not stored by the AI)

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect third-party integrations (like QuickBooks)

8. QuickBooks Integration

When you connect SmartCookBook to QuickBooks:

  • We access only the data necessary to create bills and manage vendors
  • We do not store your QuickBooks data beyond what's needed for the integration
  • You can disconnect QuickBooks at any time from the Settings page

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
