Voice Recognition - The Star Feature

Why Voice Matters

Traditional browser voice recognition has a 2-second timeout on mobile devices, making it useless for recipe dictation. SmartCookBook solves this with Google Cloud Speech V2 API - unlimited recording time, 96%+ accuracy, and automatic punctuation for perfect parsing.
